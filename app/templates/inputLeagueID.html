How to find your FPL Draft League ID for the 25/26 season?

Step 1:

Login to FPL Draft in your browser on your computer and go to your 'League' tab https://draft.premierleague.com

Step 2:

Right click on a blank part of the page and click 'Inspect Element' and go to the 'Network' tab

Step 3:

There is a filter that defaults to 'All', change this to 'XHR/Fetch'

Step 4:

In the search box, type in either 'details' or 'league' and click on the result and you should see some JSON with your league details in it

Step 5:

Towards the top of this JSON you will see an 'id' value, this is your league id. It should be just below the timezone of your draft
