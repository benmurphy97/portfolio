How to find your FPL Draft League ID for the 25/26 season?

Step 1:
Login to FPL Draft in your browser on your computer and go to your 'League' tab https://draft.premierleague.com

Step 2:
Right click on a blank part of the page and click 'Inspect Element' and go to the 'Network' tab

Step 3:
In the upper left of the Network tab there is a filter that can default to 'All', change this to 'XHR/Fetch'

Step 4:
Near this filter there is a search/filter box, type in 'details' and click on the result and you should see some text with your league details in it.

Step 5:
Towards the top of this JSON you will see an 'id' value, this is your league id. It should be just below where it says draft_tz_show.
